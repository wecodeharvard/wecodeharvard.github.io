About WECode

WECode (Women Engineers Code) is a CS conference targeting to create the next generation of technical leaders, foster a network and community among collegiate engineers, and promote more female representation in the technical industry.

Network and attend workshops headed by professionals from Palantir, Microsoft, Google, and more!

Any questions? Please e-mail harvardwecode@gmail.com.
