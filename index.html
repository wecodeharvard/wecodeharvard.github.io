About WECode

WECode (Women Engineers Code Conference) is a conference geared towards collegiate women interested in Computer Science that seeks to promote female representation in technical fields and create a community among women in technology.

Network and attend workshops headed by professionals from Palantir, Microsoft, Google, and more!
